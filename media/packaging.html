PPA Student Design Challenge Winning Entry

2020

Above is my team’s winning entry to the PPA Student Design Challenge 2020. Within our team of two, I focused on the structural concept and creation of the design. Remarkable features include single sided printing, glue-less structures, entirely paper-board and ready to ship. Our team placed first in the competition, making history for both Ryerson University and Canada.

RyePack

2020-2021

Being this year's VP of Competitions at Ryersons Packaging student group has given me the opportunity to facilitate packaging competitions for GCM students including Corrugami Logo Challenge, PPA Student Design Challenge 2021 and plans for an upcoming internal competition. Through these I have hosted workshops and 1-on-1 meetings to help develop the students skills and competition entries.



Packaging Prototyping Teaching Assitant

2020

Assisted with the implementation of in-class packaging competition which was a partnership between Nestle Turtles and GCM 808: Packaging Prototyping. In this I created course materials, tutorials, conducted feedback and idea development meetings with students and output all prototypes on campus. In addition we created new prototypes for pouches and overwrap solutions and methods of creating them to offer alternative packaging designs for the students to use.
