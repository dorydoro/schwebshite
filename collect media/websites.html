Snailnail.ca

2021

Designed the website for Launch 2.0 of local press-on-nail business, SnailNail. Her store was hosted by Shopify and I used HTML, CSS and Liquid to create the website and elements were created in Adobe Illustrator. The website and graphics used were designed to be easy to maintain and update as the business grows.
 
On the website I implemented a product customizer where consumers can easily create their own nail designs. The choices would influence the final price for the product which ensured fair compensation for the artist. Consumers could upload reference photos and comments with their order. This customizer directly increased income by 50%
 
Within the first hour of the website relaunch, SnailNail generated as much revenue as it used to in a month. Doubling within 24hrs.
